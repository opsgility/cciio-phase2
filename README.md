## How to use
Each exercise is self-contained: what you'll do, time, setup, a copy-paste prompt, a sample, follow-up prompts, a verification checklist, and a reflection. Work in **Copilot Chat** and the M365 apps with the sample files (or your own non-sensitive content). The live labs are also hosted at **github.com/opsgility/CMD-CCIIO**.

## Ground rules
Copilot drafts; you decide. **Verify before you trust** — check names, dates, dollar figures, and owners against the source. **Never paste sensitive or non-public CMS information** — use the fictional sample files or your own non-sensitive content only. Iterate: your first prompt is a starting point, not the finish line. All sample data in this repository is fictional and for training only.
